What is a mapcode?

A mapcode is a minimalist representation of any location on the Earth. Mapcodes put an emphasis on being short while still easily recognizable, memorizable, and communicable.

Who created mapcodes?

Mapcodes were created by Pieter Geelen and Harrold Goddijn in 2001.


Learn more about mapcodes at mapcode.com
